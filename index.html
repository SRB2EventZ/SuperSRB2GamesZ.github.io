Welcome to Super SRB2 Games Z!


Super SRB2 Games Z (or SSRB2GZ for short) is a revival for the old srb2 community, and it's for people who want to experience how it was like back in the day. From versions 1.09.4 to 2.1, even 2.2.
You can join the discord server and chat with other people, host netgames, or join other peoples netgames!
